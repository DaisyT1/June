Flights

Ancona

Ryanair fly directly from London Stansted to Ancona once a day. It is then around a 50 minute drive to Treia, the nearest town to the wedding. This is the easiest option.

Bologna

Ryanair - fly multiple times daily out of London Stansted and Luton.

BA - fly multiple times daily from London Heathrow.

EasyJet - fly once daily out of London Gatwick.

From Bologna it is around a 2hr45 drive to Treia, the nearest town. Or there is a train which will take you into Ancona, again around 2hr45. We recommend this for people who really don't like Ryanair, or those who are keen to try the best tortellini.

Perugia

Ryanair have a once daily flight to Perugia, it is then a 1hr40 drive to Treia.

Gothenburg to Rome

There are direct flights for those travelling from Sweden! Norwegian and Ryanair both have daily flights. From Rome, it is a 3hr drive to Treia.

Car hire

Each of these airports (including tiny Ancona) have the major car rental companies such as Hertz, Europcar and Enterprise.

A little tip we have learnt from our travels is buying standalone car hire excess insurance as it is substantially cheaper than taking insurance out through the car hire firm. We use this company, but there are plenty of others.
